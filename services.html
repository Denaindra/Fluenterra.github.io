Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our SME Consulting Services

What is an operations audit and why does my SME need one?

An operations audit is a comprehensive assessment of your business workflows, processes, team structures, and management systems. It identifies hidden inefficiencies, revenue leakage, and bottlenecks that are costing your business money. For SMEs in Sri Lanka, an operations audit provides a clear, prioritised roadmap for improvement with quantified ROI projections helping you make data-driven decisions about where to invest for maximum impact.

How long does a typical consulting engagement take?

Most engagements follow our proven four-phase methodology and deliver visible operational improvements within 90 days. A focused operations audit typically takes 2–4 weeks, while a comprehensive process improvement and implementation programme runs 8–12 weeks depending on the complexity of your organisation. We tailor every engagement to your specific needs and timelines.

What industries does Fluenterra specialise in?

Fluenterra has deep domain expertise across IT firms, government and public sector agencies, management consultancies, manufacturing companies, professional services organisations, and retail and e-commerce businesses. Our frameworks are specifically designed for the operational challenges that small and medium enterprises face across these sectors in Sri Lanka and South Asia.

How is Fluenterra different from traditional management consultants?

Unlike traditional consultants who deliver reports and recommendations, Fluenterra takes a hands-on implementation approach. We embed within your team, implement changes directly, train your staff, build the systems, and measure results. Every engagement is tied to clear KPIs and measurable ROI so you see tangible value, not just theory.

What is the cost of Fluenterra's consulting services?

Our pricing is tailored to the scope and complexity of each engagement. We offer flexible engagement models including project-based pricing, retainer arrangements, and phased implementation plans designed for SME budgets. Every engagement begins with a complimentary strategy call where we assess your needs and provide a transparent proposal with clear deliverables and expected outcomes.

Can Fluenterra help my business if we are based outside Sri Lanka?

Yes. While our headquarters are in Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, we serve SMEs across South Asia and internationally. We offer both on-site and remote consulting engagements, and our proprietary frameworks are adaptable to businesses in any market. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements and location.
